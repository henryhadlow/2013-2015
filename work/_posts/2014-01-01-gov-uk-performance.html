GOV.UK Performance — performance data for government services

Central government in the UK provides around 800 services – things like renewing a passport or booking a driving test. We're using data to help improve them.

{% include img.html width="1920" height="1080" alt="" src="gov-uk-performance/05.jpg" %}

We've identified the 4 metrics that matter for improving government services.

{% include img.html width="840" height="1080" alt="Icons for cost per transaction, user satisfaction, completion rate and digital take-up" src="gov-uk-performance/kpi-icons.png" %}

  • Cost per transaction – how much does it cost the taxpayer?
  • User satisfaction – how satisfied are the people who use it?
  • Completion rate – how many people manage to use the service successfully, and how many give up?
  • Digital take-up – how many people use the digital service, and how many use the phone or a paper form?

 

Every dashboard comes with a presentation view – so it's easy to see how your service is performing.
