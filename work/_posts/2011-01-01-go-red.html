GO RED! — professional illustrators play consequences — unique prints for a great cause

The Partners have been working with Richard House Children's Hospice on their annual fundraiser GO RED!

{% include img.html width="1920" height="1080" alt="Three GO RED! sections combined" src="go-red/01.jpg" %} {% include video.html youtube-key="2DCh4WqBw6c" title="GO RED! – YouTube" %}

We asked over a hundred professional illustrators to play a giant game of consequences, then created a website where you can mix and match their illustrations to create your own combination, which you can buy, as long as nobody else has beaten you to it!

Each print is completely unique, and the money goes to help the kids at Richard House, London's first childrens' hospice.
