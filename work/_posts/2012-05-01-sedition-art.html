seditionart.com — pioneering a new way to buy art

Artists such as Damien Hirst and Tracey Emin typically sell limited edition works in small numbers, for large sums money. s[edition] flips that model on it's head. Works are sold in much larger editions but for small sums, making art affordable for everyone.

Visit site →

Works are primarily video and interactive pieces, making full use of the digital medium.
