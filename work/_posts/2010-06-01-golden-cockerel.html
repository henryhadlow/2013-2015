The Golden Cockerel — illustrations for a dark Russian fairytale

Pushkin's ‘The Golden Cockerel’ is a children's story with a gruesome sting in the tail. I wrote a new translation of Pushkin's tale and drew illustrations. References include the formal conventions of Indian miniature paintings and the symmetry of Russian nesting dolls.
